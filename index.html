OBJECT:
You are the white square. The object of the game is to find the exit (orange tile).
POINTS:
If you find the exit in:
  • Less than or equal to 10 seconds: 200 points
  • Between 11 and 20 seconds: 100 points
  • More than 20 seconds: 50 points
Controls:
MOVEMENT: [W, A, S, D] or [Left, Up, Right, Down]
RELOAD: R
TIPS:
If it's too hard for you, set it to a lower difficulty: BEGINNER, EASY, INTERMEDIATE, or ADVANCED
